1nematch FAQ

What is a Betting Exchange?

A betting exchange is a platform where users can bet against each other, rather than betting against a traditional bookmaker. You can both back (bet for) and lay (bet against) outcomes.

How Does Onematch Work?

On Onematch, you can place bets or offer odds for other users to match. This creates a more dynamic and potentially more profitable betting experience.

Backing vs Laying

Backing means betting on something to happen, while laying means betting against something happening. This gives you more flexibility in your betting strategy.
